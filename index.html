About AllergyLocate

I built this because finding allergen information before eating somewhere shouldn't require 20 minutes of Googling. Most major restaurants already publish allergen PDFs or pages, but they're hard to find. AllergyLocate indexes them all in one place.

I'm Zamin Rizvi, a high school student with severe food allergies and a member of FARE's Teen Advisory Group. I built this in 2026. All 100+ restaurant links were verified manually by me and a team of researchers.

NJ Senate Bill S3394

This site exists partly because no law currently requires restaurants to post allergen information publicly. NJ S3394 would change that — requiring all NJ restaurants to publicly disclose allergen info for menu items.

This database is proof that restaurants already publish allergen information. The bill would make that the standard. Read the bill →

The 9 major food allergens (US)

Per the FDA: milk, eggs, fish, shellfish, tree nuts, peanuts, wheat, soybeans, and sesame. Restaurants in this database publish information about some or all of these.

A note on accuracy

We link to publicly available information, but restaurants update their menus. Always confirm allergen info directly with the restaurant before eating there. This database is a starting point, not a guarantee.
